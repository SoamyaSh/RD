What is a Recurring Deposit?

A Recurring Deposit (RD) is a type of investment where you deposit a fixed amount monthly for a specific tenure. It offers guaranteed returns, making it a safe option for achieving your financial goals.

How to Start an RD?

Starting a Recurring Deposit with us is simple and hassle-free. Follow these steps:

  • Contact us through the form in the Contact Us section or call us directly.
  • Provide basic details like your name, email, and desired monthly deposit amount.
  • We'll guide you through the documentation process, including submitting KYC documents like ID proof, address proof, and a passport-sized photo.
  • Choose your preferred tenure and make your first deposit through cash, cheque, or online transfer.
  • Once completed, we'll provide you with your RD account details and start your journey toward financial security.
